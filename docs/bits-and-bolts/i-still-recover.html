I Still Recover!

Bits and Bolts - Exploring TF:TCG Synergy

2020.06.12

Welcome to Bits and Bolts, a series intended to help Transformers the Trading Card Game newcomers discover some of the valuable interactions between different cards.

Today, let's discover a nice trick to bring a bot back from the KO pile, without having to give them back.

I'm sure you know the I Still Function! card. It allows you to bring a character back from the KO pile with 1 remaining health, as long as you return them to the KO area at the end of your turn. It's a powerful card, which is why it's devoid of battle icons. It's even more powerful when used with the mini-cassettes.

One nice synergy is with Raider Rumble. Let's say Raider Rumble is in your KO area and you play I Still Function! (commonly known as ISF) to bring him back. You ensure he's in his cassette mode, then at the end of your turn you get to his Revenge effect; it's time to do 2 damage to an opponent's character. Using an ISF to get a 2 damage may not be the best use of the card, but in Wave 5 it's become a favourite trick in Quake decks (where that I Still Function! on Rumble becomes 1 damage to everyone but Quake, and 2 damage to an opponent character of your choice).

ISF and the Mini-Cassettes is not just about Rumble however. The nicest use of I Still Function! with Blaster and Soundwave's Mini-Cassettes will work with any of the cassettes. The Blaster vs Soundwave set came with the Recover Cassette card. Recover Cassette lets you place one of the music player's cassettes back under them, additionally healing another of your characters. A nice card, though you do have to put it in the KO area after using it.

So let's imagine you've brought a cassette back onto the battlefield with an I Still Function!, and then you play Recover Cassettes. You might have used a Brainstorm to play both of those, or you might have played a Recover Cassettes as the top card of your deck when flipping Blaster. As explained in this FAQ, your recovered cassette gets to cheat the KO, and is fully healed to boot:

In a turn where I play Brainstorm, allowing for 2 more actions. I play I Still Function!, bringing Buzzsaw (Spy) from the KO Area, then I play Recover Cassette, putting Buzzsaw underneath Soundwave. Will that cancel out I Still Function!?
Yes, it will. Once Buzzsaw goes underneath Soundwave, all existing and/or pending game effects stop applying to him.

This is a rather specific piece of synergy, but there are few ways to heal a character fully, and fewer ways to return a character to the battlefield fully healed. Something to bear in mind for any Blaster or Soundwave decks.
