Bits and Bolts - Exploring TFTCG Synergy

2020.06.25

Welcome to Bits and Bolts, a series intended to help Transformers the Trading Card Game newcomers discover some of the valuable interactions between different cards. Let's discuss some synergies that plan the deck for your advantage.

Transformer modes are at the heart of the game, and Wave 5 brought a big shake up to these modes. We got two new modes, new battle cards that are mode specific, and a the first character who is in two modes at the same time. Head Mode. Body Mode. It's not Bot Mode. Combiner, Alt (1/2), Bot Mode. Battle Cards: Mission Briefing. Talk about Battlemasters, Heads, and Cassettes. Precision Fire. Mention you can't target Head mode. Hit and Run. Headbutt. Conversion Expertise. Character: Sky Shadow Combiner + Body mode.

Thank you for reading; I hope this was short, sweet, and informative.
